BIOGRAPHY

Conrado Roura was born on 28 February 1996 in Argentina, is a celebrated rugby player known for his passion and dedication to the sport. Starting his journey at local club Palermo Bajo, Roura quickly rose through the ranks, showcasing exceptional talent and a deep love for rugby.

CAREER HIGHLIGHTS

He plays as a flanker and has also showcased his skills for Peñarol in the Súper Liga Americana de Rugby and the Dallas Jackals in Major League Rugby. Roura previously represented Argentina Sevens, competing in 13 tournaments between 2017 and 2018.

LEGACY

Conrado Roura is not only an inspiring athlete but also a testament to the growth of Argentine rugby on the international stage. At just 28 years old, he continues to play professionally, currently with Rangers Vicenza in Italy. His dedication and contributions to the sport have made him a role model for aspiring rugby players, showcasing the potential of Argentine talent in global competitions.
